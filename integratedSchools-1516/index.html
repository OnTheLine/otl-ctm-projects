Integrated schools in Hartford in 2015-16
Several schools in Hartford have been targeted to improve diversity, but the large majority have not been targeted and are all still racially and ethnically isolated. Schools marked green are considered integrated, with a black and Hispanic population below 75%. Schools marked red have a black and Hispanic population of 75% or higher.
